EXCLUSIVE OPPORTUNITY FOR STEM RACING 2025 WORLD FINALISTS ​TO JOIN THE KOMATSU-WILLIAMS RACING ENGINEERING ACADEMY

We are delighted to once again partner with Komatsu-Williams Racing Engineering Academy the STEM Racing World Finals. Providing our World Finalists with a highly prized opportunity to join the academy as part of the 2026 cohort, this partnership offers students the chance to get to the heart of innovation in Formula 1.

So, if you want to experience engineering at its highest level, build connections in one of the world's most competitive industries, and learn from some of the best minds in the business, submit your application now (your exclusive application link has been sent to your email address; if you haven't received it, contact the STEM Racing team).

Applications close at midnight, August 15th 2025.

Please note, there is no cost associated with being part of the academy, this is a purely merit-based opportunity.

WHY APPLY

The academy is looking for the brightest and best, curious engineers who want to build, break and push boundaries. It’s a one-of-a-kind opportunity that combines hands-on engineering experience with personalised mentorship and exclusive industry exposure.

As an academy member, you'll receive:

  • Access to e-learning educational F1 modules and tasks
  • Engagement with your own mentor from Komatsu or Williams Racing
  • Early access to career opportunities
  • Many other cross company initiatives
  • The ability to build global networks; learning and connecting with the community.​​

HOW DO i APPLY?

The application process will consist of 2 stages:

Stage 1 -Submit your CV, cover letter and video via the link sent to your email address by STEM Racing HQ.

Applications will close at midnight on: Friday 15th August 2025

Stage 2 - Successful individuals will be invited to attend our Assessment Centre at the World Finals in Singapore.

Please note you must be present in Singapore and able to attend the assessment centre to be eligible to apply. The assessment centre will take place on Wednesday 1st October.

FAQS

The programme begins in January 2026.

How long is the academy?

This will depend upon your year of study and your home country. Our learning platform provides engineering insight over the course of three years.

Are students able to attend a race weekend?

Attending grand prix races is not guaranteed. Last year, academy members joined Atlassian Williams Racing in Qatar. Details of the schedule in Singapore will be released shortly.
