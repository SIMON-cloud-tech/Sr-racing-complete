Teachers, it's your turn to learn!

We’re thrilled to partner with Autodesk to bring you a series of FREE CPD courses designed to equip you with the skills and confidence to teach Computer Aided Design (CAD) and Computer Aided Manufacturing (CAM) using Autodesk Fusion. Developed specifically for STEM Racing, over the coming months, these hands-on, entry-level courses will empower you to bring STEM learning to life by enabling your students to design and manufacture their own STEM Racing cars — all from your classroom.

Fully funded and available at no cost to your or your school, make sure you don't miss out and book your place today.

cad fusion fundamentals

Time required: 12-hour course which runs over one full and one half day.

In easy-to-follow, live online sessions, you’ll explore the essential tools in Autodesk Fusion and gain the tools and confidence to guide your students through the car design process, from sketch to final render.

Key Learnings:

  • How to navigate the Fusion interface
  • ​2D sketching fundamentals
  • 3D modelling techniques: Extrude, Loft, Mirror
  • Building and managing assemblies
  • Creating detailed technical drawings
  • Rendering your car designs
  • Maximising Fusion’s features for speed, performance and creativity

CAM FUSION FUNDAMENTALS

​Time required? 8-hour course taking place over one full day or two after-school sessions.

In this engaging, instructor-led course, you’ll gain the skills to bring real-world manufacturing into your classroom and help your students build faster, smarter, and more competitive cars.

Key Learnings:

  • How to set up Fusion CAM and pair it with a CNC machine
  • Creating 2.5, 3, and 4-axis toolpaths
  • Simulating machining and generating G-code
  • Using Fusion effectively, even without a CNC machine
  • Tips to prep students for the Fusion ACU exam and technical judging

NEED A DIFFERENT DATE?

If you'd like to complete this free training but are unable to make any of the dates listed, you can attend a general Autodesk Fusion Fundamentals course at the links below. ​

Alternatively, if you'd like to receive a custom STEM Racing + Autodesk session, get in touch with the team today and let us know what date/s would for you and we will try our best to accommodate a new course.
