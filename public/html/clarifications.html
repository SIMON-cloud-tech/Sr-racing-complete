Official Technical & Competition Regulations - Clarification Board

Welcome to the official regulations clarification notice board.

All questions received from teams that STEM Racing Limited deem as requiring a clarification will be posted here.

T8.5.1 Nose and wing support structure location

Question:

T8.5, it states "The nose and front wing support structure must be in front of reference plane A, no more than 25.0mm above the track surface and no wider than 15mm either side of the centre line reference plane." However, the diagram attached shows 16.0mm

Answer:

Diagram updated to correct dimension from 16.0mm to 15.0mm either side of the centre line reference plane.

"The nose and front wing support structure must be in front of reference plane A, no more than 25.0mm above the track surface and no wider than 15mm either side of the centre line reference plane."
