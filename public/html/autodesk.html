Autodesk empowers STEM RACING participants and academic institutions around the world with free access to the same cutting-edge design software used by industry professionals. From hands-on learning resources to engaging competitions and skill-building programs, Autodesk offers valuable support for both educators and students. Equip your team with professional-grade tools—and watch innovation come to life.

TEACHERS - How to register your students?

The Autodesk Education plan has been updated to enable educators to get a class of students quickly and easily up and running with Autodesk products for educational use. Eligible educators can use this enhancement to access 125 single-user subscriptions for each Autodesk product available through the Education plan.

​The first step to using this enhancement is to choose the products that your class will use on the Class/Lab tab of the Get Products page.Then, you’ll use the User Management section of your Autodesk Account to assign product access to your students.By onboarding your class this way, your students will not have to confirm their own eligibility for educational access.

Click here to check if your school is currently in the list of eligible schools. If not you can submit a quick application to become verified.

This enhancement is an additional way to access Autodesk products for educational use and does not change the other ways that your students have for accessing Autodesk products. So, if your students prefer to confirm their own eligibility for educational access, they may continue to do so.
